Crazy Buffalo Sauce Recipes, Hold The Wings
The real truth is that we love Buffalo sauce. That neon orange, spicy, comforting sauce that feels so bad but so right at the same time. We are, guilty as charged, huge fans. Football season? Sure! As long as Buffalo sauce is involved.
We love Buffalo sauce so much that we often want to eat it even when we are tired of eating chicken wings. Luckily, there is a solution: eating buffalo sauce on everything, not just wings. So that you too can enjoy the glory of buffalo sauce beyond the chicken, we rounded up 31 indulgent recipes featuring everything from mac and cheese to deviled eggs smothered in the good stuff. There are even a few with no chicken present at all, so vegetarians can enjoy the majesty of Buffalo sauce, too. You hear that chicken wings? You're not the boss of us anymore.
