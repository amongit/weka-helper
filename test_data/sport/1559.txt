Christian Horner reflects on 'phenomenal' year for Red Bull
Red Bull team principal Christian Horner talks to BBC Sport's Jake Humphrey and Eddie Jordan as his team finish with a one-two at the the final race of the season, the Brazilian Grand Prix.
Mark Webber finished ahead of team-mate Sebastian Vettel for his only victory of the season and Horner says it caps an "incredible year for the team".
