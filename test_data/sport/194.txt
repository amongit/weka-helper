Soccer-Atletico v Leverkusen goes to extra time
MADRID, March 17 (Reuters) - Atletico Madrid's Champions League last-16 second leg at home to Bayer Leverkusen will go into extra time after the Spanish team led 1-0 after 90 minutes to leave the sides locked at 1-1 on aggregate on Tuesday. (Reporting by Iain Rogers, editing by Tony Jimenez)
