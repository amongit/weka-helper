Westwood enjoying mixing with film stars at Dunhill Links
Former champion Lee Westwood explains to BBC Radio 5 live's Iain Carter why he enjoys playing in the Alfred Dunhill Links Championship.
The world number two says mixing with celebrities from the world of film and sport makes the pro-am tournament "different from other weeks on tour".
