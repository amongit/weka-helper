2012 NFL Draft Round 7 Results: Justin Anderson, Michael Smith Among Picks
The 2012 NFL Draft has come to an end.
Indianapolis kicked off the final round by grabbing a blocker for No. 1 overall pick Andrew Luck by selecting guard Justin Anderson of Georgia. Then they ended the draft by drafting a backup for the rookie quarterback by selecting Chandler Harnish out of Illinois.
Tampa Bay added another running back after taking Boise State's Doug Martin in the first round, while Boise State quarterback Kellen More went undrafted.
Did any teams get any steals in the final round of the draft? Scroll down for the rest of the picks from the seventh round.
