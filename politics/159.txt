A daily look at late-breaking news, coming events and stories that will be talked about in Pennsylvania on Monday.
FOUNDATIONS FUNDING $11 MILLION FOR PUBLIC SPACE IMPROVEMENTS IN PHILADELPHIA
The James L. Knight and William Penn foundations are putting money toward five Philadelphia projects as part of an initiative led by the city’s Fairmount Park Conservancy to activate public spaces.

GUN RIGHTS SUPPORTERS CHALLENGE SUBURBAN PHILADELPHIA GUN ORDINANCE
Several dozen people gathered at Bala Cynwyd park in Lower Merion Township yesterday. The rule doesn’t ban guns from parks, but organizers contend that it is vaguely written and could mislead gun owners into thinking that firearms are banned.
