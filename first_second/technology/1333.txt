Apple has announced that its highly anticipated Apple Watch will be available in nine countries on April 24 at a recommended retail price averaging higher than the Apple iPhone 6.
The Apple Watch will be made available in Australia, Canada, China, France, Germany, Hong Kong, Japan, the UK, and the US by reservation in Apple's retail stores, select Apple authorised resellers in China and Japan, and the online Apple store. Select department stores and boutiques in major cities, including Colette in Paris, Dover Street Market in London and Tokyo, Maxfield in Los Angeles, and The Corner in Berlin, will also be selling the watch.
