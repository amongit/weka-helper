Retired heavyweight boxer David Haye tells Hardtalk he'll lower his wage demands to fight one of the Klitschko brothers in the new year and that talks are "going on".
The interview is scheduled to broadcast on Monday December 5 on World at 0430 GMT, 0930 GMT, 1530 GMT & 2130GMT and on the News Channel at 0430 on December 5 and 0030 on December 6.
