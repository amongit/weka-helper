Austin, Texas (CNN)CNN caught up with Rep. Darrell Issa, R- California, at Austin's notorious SXSW interactive festival. Among the many things discussed were Hillary Clinton's email controversy. While Issa previously addressed the emails on CNN's 'State of the Union,' he's now making a new comparison, likening "email-gate" to Watergate.
"She is beginning to look like a modern, Democratic, Richard Nixon," said the former House Oversight Committee chairman. "That's not good for somebody who aspires to be president."
