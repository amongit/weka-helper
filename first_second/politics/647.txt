Strong holiday retail sales helped Apple Inc. reach gargantuan sales figures during the first quarter of its 2015 fiscal year.
In the three-month period ending Dec. 27, Apple sold a record 74.5 million iPhones, a record 5.5 million Macs and another 21.4 million iPads. Its quarterly revenue reached a personal-best $74.6 billion, with net income in excess of $18 billion.
