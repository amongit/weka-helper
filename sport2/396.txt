Van on fire next to Olympic Stadium
By Nicola Harley
8:29AM GMT 29 Oct 2014
A large plume of smoke rising from the Olympic Stadium sparking fears the venue was on fire turned out to be from a van fire.
London Fire Brigade sent one engine to reports of a fire at Marshgate Lane, Stratford, next to the venue.
It turned out to be a van on fire and the flames were quickly extinguished.
Scotland Yard said it was not involved in the incident.
