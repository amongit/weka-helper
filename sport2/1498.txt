Women's British Open - Caroline Masson dazzles on day three
Germany's Caroline Masson opens up a two-shot lead at the Women's British Open on day three, finishing 15 under par overall.
The 22-year-old from Dusseldorf had never led an event before and says Martin Kaymer, who she shares a coach with, has been an inspiration to her.
Reigning champion Yani Tseng lies second going into the fourth and final day at Carnoustie, with Scotland's Catriona Matthew in joint third.
