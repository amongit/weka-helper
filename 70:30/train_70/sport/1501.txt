Meena Lee takes day-one lead at Women's British Open
South Korea's Meena Lee shoots a seven-under-par 65 to lead after the first round of the Women's British Open at Carnoustie.
Lee, 29, carded seven birdies in a blemish-free round to finish two shots clear of American Brittany Lincicome.
The Korean admits she was "lucky" on the 18th when her ball bounced over the stream and is confident she can continue her good form into the weekend.
