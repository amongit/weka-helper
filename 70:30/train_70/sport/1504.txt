Nicky Hayden and Valentino Rossi seek happier 2012 for Ducati
Ducati rider Nicky Hayden talks about his recovery from a broken wrist, revealing he hopes to be fit to take part in the pre-season test in Malaysia at the end of January.
However, the 2006 world champion says he will not be 100% as he looks to make amends for a disappointing 2011.
Team-mate Valentino Rossi also talks about his hopes for 2012 and says he would like to sign one more contract before possibly calling time on his trophy-laden career in 2014 .
