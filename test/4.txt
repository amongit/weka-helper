AustraliaWOW! gets business into shape with mobility (full interview)
Nathan Hague, founder and director of SaaS business AustraliaWOW!, shares some of his experiences following the business's move away from physical infrastructure to a completely virtual, mobile-powered operation. Hague, whose business delivers software and services to around 30,000 fitness instructors worldwide, says he was surprised how easy the change has been.
