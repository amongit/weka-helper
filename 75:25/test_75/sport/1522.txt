Amir Khan surprised by birthday cake
Coverage: Live commentary: BBC Radio 5 live early hours of Sunday, 11 December; re-run BBC 5 live Sports Extra at 0800, 0900 & 1000 GMT. Live television: Full card from 2000 GMT live on Sky Sports 1
IBF and WBA light welterweight champion Amir Khan is surprised the cake presented to him on his 25th birthday by fight promoter Oscar de la Hoya and his trainer Freddie Roach.
The Bolton-born Olympic silver medallist defends his world titles against Lamont Peterson at the Convention Center, Washington DC in the early hours of Sunday morning.
