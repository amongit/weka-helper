A fast-food eater decided to pop into McDonald’s to have a bite to eat when he came across quite a startling sight.
As Justin Irwin soon discovered, the staff were obviously enthralled by the Super Bowl - one employee in particular.
McDonald's employee appears upset at the Seattle Seahawks' loss. Credit: Storyful / YouTube / Justin Irwin
It appeared that the fast food chain worker in question is a Seattle Seahawks fan, since she looked and sounded very displeased with the American Football result.
The Seahawks lost 24 - 28 against the New England Patriots in Sunday's Super Bowl final.
