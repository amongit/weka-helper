Jenson Button wary of wet race conditions
McLaren's Jenson Button says that rain forecast for Sunday's Brazilian Grand Prix may not be beneficial to him.
Button says the Interlagos circuit is "very tricky" in wet conditions as the car moves around so much.
The 2009 world champion will start the race third on the grid behind the Red Bull's of Mark Webber and Sebastian Vettel.
