Kingsbury wins dual moguls World Cup event
INAWASHIRO, Japan (AP) -- Olympic silver medalist Mikael Kingsbury of Canada won a World Cup dual moguls event on Sunday.
Kingsbury beat Japan's Sho Endo to claim his fourth World Cup title of the season and move into first place in the overall standings ahead of compatriot Alex Bilodeau. Pascal-Olivier Gagne, also of Canada, was third.
Sochi bronze medalist Hannah Kearney of the United States won the women's event, beating Elena Muratova of Russia for her fourth World Cup title of the season. Japan's Junko Hoshino was third.
Olympic gold medalist Justine Dufour-Lapointe of Canada, who won Saturday's moguls event, placed ninth.
