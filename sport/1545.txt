Highlights - Kenya's Leonard Komon wins Great South Run
Watch highlights of the 16km Great South Run on the streets of Southsea as Leonard Komon of Kenya takes the honours in the men's race with a time of 46 minutes and 18 seconds, falling to better Haile Gebrselassie's world 10mile record of 44.23.
Ethiopia's former world half-marathon runner up Asselefech Mergia wins the women's race in 52 minutes and 55 seconds, as 24,000 people pound the streets of Portsmouth in the 22nd hosting of the annual event.
