George Griffin and Jordan Hand join Wakefield on loan
George Griffin could make his debut for Wakefield against Huddersfield on Sunday
18 March 2015 Last updated at 13:43
Wakefield Trinity Wildcats have signed Salford Red Devils forward George Griffin and St Helens prop Jordan Hand on one-month loan deals.
Both players could make their debuts for the Wildcats in Sunday's game against Huddersfield.
Griffin, 22, joined Salford from relegated London Broncos for this season, but is yet to feature for them.
Hand, 21, has been on loan at Championship side Whitehaven this season.
