Floyd Mayweather and Manny Pacquiao will go head-to-head on May 2 in Las Vegas after years of negotiations.
Mayweather, worth $280 million, has never been beaten in a fight and weighs 3kg more than Pacquiao at 68kg.
Pacquiao or "Pac Man" has lost five times professionally and has a net worth of $100million.
Floyd Mayweather and Manny Pacquiao
Pacquiao started boxing at the age of 16 being paid $2 per match for a local TV show in the Philipines called "Blow By Blow".
Later in life, he ran for congress where he got elected twice and now is building a two-storey church in General Santos City - due to open December 2015.
Mayweather was born in to a family of boxers and started at the age of nine.
The cheapest tickets on sale for the big head-to-head at the MGM Grand Garden Arena Hotel, Las Vegas, start at $1,250 to $6000 at the ring side. For those desperate for a ticket, they are also available on the black market at around $100,000.
