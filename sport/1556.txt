Australia's Sally Pearson blazes to 100m hurdles record
Australian Sally Pearson sets the fastest time in a major championships and wins gold in the 100m hurdles at the World Championships in Daegu.
Pearson clocked 12.28 seconds in a stunning performance. Britain's Tiffany Porter missed out on a medal finishing fourth.
