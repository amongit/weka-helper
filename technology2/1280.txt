Dual tone HTC One E9+ with quad HD display appears on HTC China site
There have been rumors of more HTC One devices, in addition to the One M9, and it looks like the first official one comes to us from China as the HTC One E9+.
The last E series device, the HTC One E8, has a cool dual curved design, 5 inch 1080p display, 13 megapixel rear camera, 5 megapixel front facing camera, and Snapdragon 801 processor.
The HTC One E9+ is very similar to the HTC One M9 with a dual tone metal body, 20 megapixel rear camera, front facing UltraPixel camera, and BoomSound stereo speakers with Dolby Audio.
Difference include a 5.5 inch WQHD display at 1440x2560 pixel resolution, MediaTek octa core processor, 2GB of RAM, and integrated 16GB storage.
There is no information on US availability at this time.
