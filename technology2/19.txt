Pioneering 3D touch screens unveiled by Lancaster University
Media playback is unsupported on your device
14 April 2015 Last updated at 08:21 BST
Scientists from Lancaster University are working on a project designed to create responsive 3D computer screens.
They have devised a way of making data "come out" of the screen.
The technology is being displayed at a major conference in South Korea this week.
