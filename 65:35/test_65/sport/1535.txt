Amir Khan and Zab Judah weigh-in on the 140lbs limit
Both Amir Khan and Zab Judah are bang on the 140lbs limit at the weigh-in for their light-welterweight unification clash in Las Vegas.
Khan, the WBA title-holder, is hoping to claim New Yorker Judah's IBF belt in the early hours of Sunday morning.
