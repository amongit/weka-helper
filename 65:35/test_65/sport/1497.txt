Women's British Open - Yani Tseng's shot of day three
Taiwan's Yani Tseng eagles the 14th at the Women's British Open on her way to a 66 - the best round of day three.
The reigning champion trails Germany's Caroline Masson by two shots going into the fourth and final day at Carnoustie.
Tseng says she is going to take an aggressive approach to defend her title on Sunday.
