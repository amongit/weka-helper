Highlights - Brazilian Grand Prix qualifying
Sebastian Vettel storms to a record-breaking 15th pole position of 2011 at the season-ending Brazilian Grand Prix, surpassing Nigel Mansell's record set in 1992 with Williams.
Lining up alongside him on the grid will be his Red Bull team-mate Mark Webber, with the McLaren pair of Jenson Button and Lewis Hamilton sharing the second row.
