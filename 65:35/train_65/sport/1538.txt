Heavyweight division not in crisis - Klitschko brothers
Wladimir Klitschko and his brother Vitali tell the BBC's Russian Service that the heavyweight division is not at a low ebb, citing the 50,000 fans who watched Wladimir's bout with David Haye.
Wladimir defeated Haye in Germany on 2 July to add Haye's WBA belt to the IBF and WBO titles he already held.
WBC champion Vitali, talks about his forthcoming bout against Tomasz Adamek and says he will not underestimate his opponent.
