Sport Explained: The Panini World Cup sticker album obsession
11:01AM BST 29 May 2014

In this week's edition of Sport Explained, Thom Gibbs explains the heady phenomenon which grips grown men, women and children alike every time a World Cup comes around: the Panini World Cup sticker album.
Not to be confused with the toasted Italian sandwich of the same name (easily done, as Thom proves), the Panini sticker album is almost as exciting as the World Cup itself. No really. Watch the video to find out why.
