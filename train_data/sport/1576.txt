British trio boxing for World Championship gold
Britain's Luke Campbell, Andrew Selby and Anthony Joshua will all fight for gold following an historic day of success at the World Amateur Boxing Championships in Baku.
English bantamweight Campbell edged a semi-final against Ireland John Joe Nevin whilst Super-heavyweight Anthony Joshua progresses after stopping Germany's Erik Pfeifer in round one.
While Selby becomes the first Welshman to contest a World final after the flyweight defeated Uzbek Jasurbek Latipov.
