UK Snooker Championship: Ding century sees off Stevens
Two-time champion Ding Junhui records a break of 133, the 224th century break of his career, on his way to a 6-5 win over Mark Stevens of Wales at the UK Championship in York.
The 24-year-old Chinese star led 5-3 before the Welshman pegged him back to 5-5.
Stevens had the first clear chance in the decider, but missed a black off its spot allowing Ding to take the frame with a break of 62.
