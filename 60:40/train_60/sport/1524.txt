Manny Pacquiao defends controversial win over Juan Manuel Marquez
World champion Manny Pacquiao defends the decision that saw him retain his WBO welterweight title with a split points win over Juan Manuel Marquez .
The Filipino was favoured by two judges with a third unable to separate the fighters as their third meeting ended with irate Marquez fans raining cans and bottles on the ring.
