London 2012: Selby and Yafai ready for British 'box-off'
British boxers Andrew Selby and Khalid Yafai speak to BBC Sport's Ronald McIntosh ahead of their best-of-three 'box-off' to decide who will claim the flyweight place at the 2012 Olympics.
The amateur fighters will clash at the Great Britain Championships in Bethnal Green, London on 11 and 12 November.
Selby says it is his "time to shine" as he deserves the Olympic spot, while Yafai claims he is the "underdog" and wants to prove his critics wrong.
Watch Selby v Yafai during BBC One's coverage of the Great Britain Championships on Saturday, 12 November, 1430-1600 GMT.
