GB boxer Anthony Joshua stops opponent to reach final
Highlights of the semi-finals of the World Amateur Boxing Championships in Azerbaijan where three Britons have made it through to the final.
Bantamweight Luke Campbell edges a tight contest to guarantee himself at least a silver medal , before Andrew Selby contests in the 52kg category.
Super-heavyweight Anthony Joshua stops his German opponent in the opening round to reach the final in his debut championships.
And light-welterweight Tom Stalker's run comes to an end after he loses to Ukraine's Denys Berinchyk in the semi-final
